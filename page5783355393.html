TtDT - Report - Appendix - Reference Iconography - Report

This appendix presents the iconography used in the report; it follows a main structure in three (four ? add interdiagram mapping?) parts:

  • pipeline architecture icons

    • Pipe-related icons

    • Filter-related icons

    • Pipeline icons

    • Nesting icons

  • life history icons

    • Identity tracking/tracing - general

    • Identity tracking/tracing - in life history space-time diagrams

  • icons common to the two areas

Each leaf in the structure is organised in two sub-sections:

  • a presentation of atomic icons

  • a presentation of composite icons, i.e. icons that are obtained through a combination of atomic icons

Within these sub-sections:

  • icons are listed according to the alphabetic order of their names.

Where appropriate, leaf or main sections are complemented with examples of diagrams that illustrate the in-context use of icons.
